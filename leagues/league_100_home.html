BNN Top 100 Prospects List Published
Friday, January 1st, 2021
The season is about to start, so it's time to publish the annual BNN Top 100 Prospects list. This year 22-year-old CF Alex Diaz of the Jersey City Mob is the #1 prospect overall.

Here is the top 10:
1) CF Alex Diaz, 22, Jersey City Mob
2) 3B Greg Mansfield, 20, Rainbow City Rockets
3) RHP Adam Seter, 20, Orlando White Caps
4) LF Fidel Garrido, 18, Portland Chinooks
5) LF Bryan Tuggle, 17, Lincoln Cornhuskers
6) RHP Dave Rodriguez, 20, Tucson Heat
7) RHP Felipe Hernandez, 20, Atomic City Fire
8) CF David Williams, 19, El Paso Stampede
9) 1B Ruben Sauceda, 20, Bozeman Bombers
10) LHP Mike Lingenfelter, 20, Rainbow City Rockets

You can view the full list in the Top Prospects Report from the reports menu. The Top Minor League Systems Report has been updated as well.
